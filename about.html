Our Story

Building bridges between global standards and African realities

Founded in Rwanda, ADC Rwanda was established to bridge the gap between global development standards and the realities of African markets. Our multidisciplinary team brings experience from international organizations, financial institutions, and local enterprises.


We understand the unique challenges and opportunities that define the African business landscape, and we're committed to providing practical, impactful solutions that drive real change.
